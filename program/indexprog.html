Program

Parlamerica will pilot and run its foreign language program in collaboration with a targeted school district. The program will be located in the individual elementary schools of that school district. Parlamerica will raise the funds to hire the foreign language teachers, create the curriculum for the program, and provide all the technical equipment and supplies required for the successful implementation of the program. The foreign language program is considered a FLES program: Foreign Language in Elementary School. Students study a foreign language during the school day as part of the in-school curriculum. The lower amount of time for study per week is 90 minutes per week:

1. 3 times/week, 30 minutes/lesson

2. 5 times/week, 15 minutes/lesson

The higher amount of time for study per week is 150 minutes per week:

1. 3 times/week, 50 minutes/lesson

2. 5 times/week, 30 minutes/lesson
